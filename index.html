Marching Spotlight!

One way to get better at Marching Band is by watching other Marching Bands that possess the "next-level" skills that we are trying to obtain! Marching Spotlight features various spectacular High School Marching Bands, College Bands, DCI groups, and possibly even WGI performances!

 

Year: 2025

Show Name:

Group: Phanton Regiment

Location: DCI World Championships

Level: DCI

Context: Intentionally untitled, Phantom Regiment's 2025 production was a deeply personal and interpretive experience rather than a defined narrative. The corps focused on raw emotion and individual meaning, inviting the audience to have their own interpretations of the performance. Their repertoire, filled with lyrical selections and intense ballads, creates a sense of reflection, struggle, and release, ultimately earning them a 6th-place finish at world finals.
